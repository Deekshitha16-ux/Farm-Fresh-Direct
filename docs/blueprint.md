# **App Name**: Farm Fresh Direct

## Core Features:

- Farmer Onboarding: Allow farmers to register and create profiles with their farm details and product listings.
- Produce Listing: Enable farmers to list their fresh produce with detailed descriptions, images, and available quantities.
- Customer Ordering: Allow customers to browse available produce, add items to their cart, and place orders directly from farmers.
- Real-time Inventory: Implement a real-time database to manage and display accurate inventory levels.
- Secure Transactions: Integrate secure payment processing for seamless and safe transactions between farmers and customers.
- Rating System: Implement a rating and review system for customers to provide feedback on their purchases.
- Blog Section: AI-powered tool helps farmers to maintain a blog, suggesting what content would be attractive to customers of different segments and regions

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Fresh green (#8FBC8F) to evoke a sense of nature and freshness.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a neutral and earthy backdrop.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700), used sparingly for highlights and calls to action, providing a sense of warmth and value.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' sans-serif for headings, and 'Alegreya' serif for body text to create a balance of elegance and readability.
- Use clean, simple icons related to farming and fresh produce.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout to showcase produce effectively.
- Subtle transitions and animations when browsing and adding items to the cart.
